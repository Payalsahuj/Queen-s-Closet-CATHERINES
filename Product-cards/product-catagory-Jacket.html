WHERE CAN I FIND FLATTERING PLUS SIZE JACKETS?

Whether youre looking for a functional puffer coat or a pocket-heavy hoodie, we make it simple to find stylish plus size jackets that match your body shape and biggest preferences. Discover lightweight overpieces, denim jackets, statement blazers, polished peacoats, fitted trench coats, collarless motos, reversible raincoats, and more stunning styles with endless details and features like longer lengths and stretchy material.

WHAT ARE SOME POPULAR STYLES OF PLUS SIZE JACKETS?

From fleece-lined pockets to adjustable drawstrings, our plus size jackets are designed with features that provide comfort and confidence as the temperatures drop.

Serving as a layering piece for cooler mornings and evenings, our Denim Barn Jacket is an especially popular choice. Its made with flexible material that resists stretching, and the bourbon wash button-front style pairs wonderfully with all of your favorite fall outfits. It even has three patch pockets for additional convenience, but the best part? The relaxed yet structured silhouette offers a just-right blend of comfort and style.

If youre searching for plus size coats with a touch of sophistication, look no further than our Leather Swing Coat. Ideal for complementing your favorite tunics, blouses, and sweaters, the trapeze silhouette is a knockout. You can choose from ivory, blue, black, grey, purple, green, and red colors, and this stylish coat ends just above the knee for coverage and coziness on colder days.

DO YOU SELL PLUS SIZE PARKAS?

Bundle up and make a statement this winter with one of our plus size parkas. Take your pick from trendy faux leather, weather-ready side snaps, cozy wool styles, and more.

Stay protected from the elements in style with our 4-Pocket High Low Parka, available in hunter green and black. These water-resistant plus size coats feature faux leather piping and a detachable fur-trimmed hood, adding a dash of trendy flair to your cold weather ensemble. The park also features rib storm cuffs and quilted lining, which are designed to leave you snug and secure. Plus, the curved back complements your shape, and the elastic bungee drawstring at the waist makes it easy to achieve the best possible fit.
